Our Mission

Provide the most up-to-date and credible information on emissions trading, GHG market activity and market-based solutions to climate change.

Our Focus

At the international level, NEXTON focuses on the developing transparency rules and accounting standards under Articles 5 and 6 of the Paris Agreement, the Carbon Offset and Reduction Scheme for International Aviation and voluntary markets.

Our Principles

Emissions trading is one of the principal policy instruments available to manage industrial greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions by encouraging operational excellence and the deployment of new and existing technologies.

Our Members

Membership to NEXTON is open to companies, business organizations and affiliated national and regional trading associations

About Nexton

In June 2010, a visionary group of international companies and business associations founded NEXTON. Their objective was to build on the economic mechanisms created by the Geneva Protocol in order to achieve climate objectives with minimal economic harm. When trading in greenhouse gases was little known or understood, NEXTON acted as a news gatherer and distributor, and assisted in developing trading clearance procedures. As the market matured, commercial entities took on these roles. NEXTON began focusing as a forum for policy dialogue, professional development and market readiness activities. It became and remains to this day the leading international advocacy voice for emissions markets.
